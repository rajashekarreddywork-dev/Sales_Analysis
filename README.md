# Sales_Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview
This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of company sales and revenue performance across markets, customers, and products. It highlights top contributors, revenue trends, and customer sales quantities, enabling data-driven insights into growth opportunities and business success factors.

---

## 🎯 Goals
- Monitor overall revenue and sales quantity.  
- Identify high-performing markets, customers, and products.  
- Track revenue trends over time to spot growth and decline phases.  
- Support business intelligence and decision-making with clear visualizations.  

---

## 🗂️ Data Source
- SQL scripts used to create and manage the database.  
- Raw and processed datasets integrated into the dashboard.  
- Visualizations built using BI tools for clarity and actionable insights.  

---

## 📌 Dashboard Highlights
- **Revenue Metrics:** Total revenue of 984.81M and sales quantity of 2429K.  
- **Market Insights:** Delhi North leads with 519.51M revenue, followed by Mumbai and Ahmedabad.  
- **Customer Analysis:** Electricalsara St. contributes 68.79% of revenue, dominating overall sales.  
- **Product Performance:** Top products include Prod040, Prod159, and Prod065.  
- **Revenue Trends:** Line chart shows fluctuations from 2017–2020, with peaks in mid-2018 and mid-2019.  

---

## 🔑 Key Insights
- Revenue is concentrated in a few major markets and customers.  
- A single customer (Electricalsara St.) accounts for the majority of revenue, indicating dependency risk.  
- Product-level analysis reveals gaps, with significant revenue attributed to unclassified items.  
- Declining trend post-2019 signals the need for strategic interventions.  

---

## 📂 Repository Structure
- `Analysis/` → SQL scripts and queries for data preparation.  
- `DataVis/` → Dashboard files and visualizations.  
- `Insight_Snapshot/` → Exported charts and key findings.

---

## ✅ Recommendations
- Diversify customer base to reduce dependency on a single client.  
- Strengthen product categorization to improve clarity in performance tracking.  
- Focus on sustaining growth in top-performing markets while addressing weaker regions.
